About us

Undergrunnen is a company that focuses on under represented Norwegian based artists and is based in two cities, Oslo and Trondheim. Our services are pretty much versatile; from promo video production to linking artists with venues and promoting their live shows via video clips. Our group is formed by five people. Three based in Oslo, and two based in Trondheim. The ones in Oslo are Sam Roman, Juno Jensen, and Espen Wik and the ones in Trondheim are Jørgen Nygård Varpe and Sepehr Haghighi.

Sam Roman

A 32 year old hailing from UK, and now living in Oslo, Sam ‘Dr’ Roman is primarily an electronic music producer and performer, specializing in Ableton live, with experience founding and promoting music events and groups in both London and UK. Sam also has studied journalism & Photography in London.

Espen Wik

A video producer, photographer and editor, located in Beitostølen, Norway. He has background from NRK, TVNorge and independent production companies. Espen has a former music education from The University of Oslo, The Music Conservatory in Buskerud and The Music Conservatory in Oslo.

Juno Jensen

A Dreamer about the universe - composing, producing, recording, singing and performing; Juno has 16 years of experience as a singer-songwriter, and is the co-founder of KOSO, a female-driven initiative for collaboration and recruiting. Always curious, Juno wants to investigate the sounds from a space probe currently orbiting Jupiter, and use these sounds to create new instruments.

Sepehr Haghighi

A musician, sound artist, engineer, graduated from the Music Production and Recording Techniques post-graduate course; University of Stavanger, Norway. He also has a bachelor degree in Drama - Acting. His personal music project goes by the name Surplus ; His area of expertise, despite being an artist, is in music production, engineering, live sound & recording and surround sound.

Jørgen Nygård Varpe

A 26 year old from Førde; Norway. He has a Bachelor’s degree both in Electronics Engineering and Music Technology, has a background from playing bass guitar in bands, and has in the recent years worked with music production, recording and sound design. Jørgen also has experience with programming for web, sound and Arduino.
